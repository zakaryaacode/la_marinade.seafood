Our Story

About
La Marinade

Where It Began

Passion for the
Deep Blue

La Marinade was born from a simple yet powerful belief: that seafood, when treated with respect and creativity, becomes poetry on a plate. Founded in Algiers, we set out to create a restaurant unlike anything the city had seen — a place where the ocean's finest gifts meet the artistry of haute cuisine.

Every ingredient is hand-selected. Every dish is a dialogue between tradition and innovation. Every visit is designed to be unforgettable — from the first glance at our 3D menu to the final lingering taste of dessert.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Ocean Freshness

We work directly with local fishermen to ensure every ingredient arrives at its absolute peak of freshness, every single day.

Sustainability

We are committed to responsible sourcing and eco-conscious practices, protecting the ocean that gives us so much.

Culinary Excellence

Inspired by the world's finest kitchens, our chefs push the boundaries of seafood cuisine with creativity and precision.

Innovation

From our pioneering 3D AR menu to our ever-evolving seasonal offerings, we embrace technology and creativity alike.

Heartfelt Hospitality

Every guest is family. We strive to make each visit personal, warm, and deeply memorable — from the first welcome to the final farewell.

Global Inspiration

Drawing from Mediterranean, Asian, and French culinary traditions, our menu is a world voyage expressed through seafood.

Our Mission

Redefining
Seafood Dining

Our mission is to make premium seafood dining an accessible luxury — a world-class experience delivered with warmth, authenticity, and a deep respect for the ocean's bounty.

Through our innovative 3D menu, guests can explore every dish before ordering — a seamless fusion of technology and gastronomy that we believe is the future of fine dining.
